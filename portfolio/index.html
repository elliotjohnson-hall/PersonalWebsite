NHS Sctoland Open Prescribing Data

Public Health Scotland publish a whole raft of freely accessible datasets that are super interesting! Here, I was inspired by a recent publication looking at the effects of Covid-19 restrictions on access to contraception in England. I decided to do a similar analysis to see how things changed in Scotland. Here, you can see all contraceptive prescription data from October 2015 to August 2022.
